## What it is

Bonne Nuit is an in-progress wind-down companion. The goal is to help someone end the day with a little more intention, without making sleep feel like productivity software.

## Why I am building it

Most habit and wellness products default to streaks, pressure, and dashboards. That can work for some things, but it feels wrong for rest. Bonne Nuit is an attempt to make the interaction smaller, calmer, and easier to keep.

## Product decisions

- Keep the interface quiet.
- Avoid punitive streak mechanics.
- Make completion feel like closure, not performance.

## Technical notes

The interesting work is in restraint: small interaction details, local-first state, and an interface that does not beg for attention.
