## What it is

quebec.run is a focused directory for the local running scene in Quebec City: clubs, events, and routes in one place.

## Why I built it

Running communities are often fragmented across social platforms, club pages, old race calendars, and word of mouth. That makes it harder for someone new to the city, or new to running, to find the right next run.

## Product decisions

- Lead with the map and local discovery instead of generic content.
- Keep the information dense enough for repeat use.
- Design for scanning: location, cadence, and fit matter more than long descriptions.

## Technical notes

The product is mostly about clean information architecture. The hard part is making local, changing data feel stable and easy to compare without turning the site into a heavy app shell.

## Status

The site is live and continues to evolve around the local running community.
