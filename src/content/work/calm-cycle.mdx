## What it is

Calm Cycle is a private period tracker for iPhone. It is deliberately simple: one-time purchase, no account, no ads, and no cloud sync.

## Why I built it

Health tracking is intimate. A period tracker should not need a growth funnel, an ad model, or an account system to be useful. The product decision was to remove as much trust overhead as possible.

## Product decisions

- Keep data on the device.
- Make privacy a product behavior, not just copy.
- Prefer a small paid app over an attention-driven business model.

## Technical notes

The implementation follows the product promise: local state, native iOS patterns, and no backend dependency for core tracking.

## Status

Calm Cycle shipped as a focused iOS product and remains the clearest example of the kind of small, privacy-respecting utility I like building.
